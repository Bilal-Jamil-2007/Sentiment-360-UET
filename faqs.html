Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Sentiment 360

What is Sentiment 360? +

Sentiment 360 is an advanced sentiment analysis platform that helps you understand public opinions from social media, product reviews, news articles, and more. It uses hybrid AI models trained on millions of samples to provide accurate sentiment insights in real-time.

How accurate is the sentiment analysis? +

Our sentiment analysis achieves 99% accuracy thanks to our hybrid models trained on millions of samples. We continuously improve our algorithms using machine learning to ensure the highest level of accuracy for your analyses.

What sources can I analyze? +

You can analyze sentiments from multiple sources including Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, Amazon reviews, Google reviews, movie platforms, news articles, and forum discussions. Our multi-source capability gives you comprehensive insights across all major platforms.

How fast are the results? +

Our platform delivers results in just 50 milliseconds on average. With our optimized infrastructure and advanced algorithms, you get instant sentiment analysis without waiting. This means you can make quick decisions based on real-time data.

Is my data secure and private? +

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All data is analyzed locally with proper encryption and security measures. We comply with international data protection standards and never share your data with third parties. Your privacy is our top priority.

Do you offer a free trial? +

Yes! We offer a free trial period so you can explore all features risk-free. No credit card required to get started. You can analyze up to 100 opinions during your trial and experience the full power of Sentiment 360.

Can I export the analysis reports? +

Absolutely! You can export detailed sentiment analysis reports in multiple formats including PDF, Excel, and CSV. Our reports include comprehensive visualizations, charts, and actionable insights that you can easily share with your team.

What kind of support do you provide? +

We provide 24/7 customer support through multiple channels including email, live chat, and help documentation. Our support team is always ready to help you with any questions or issues. Premium plans include dedicated account management.

Can I integrate Sentiment 360 with other tools? +

Yes, we offer API access and integrations with popular tools. You can integrate with your existing workflow using our REST API, webhooks, and pre-built connectors. Custom integrations are also available for enterprise customers.

What languages does Sentiment 360 support? +

We support analysis in 25+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, and more. Our multilingual models ensure accurate sentiment analysis regardless of the language of your source data.
